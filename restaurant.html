Our Story

A Passion
for Perfection

"Cooking is not just about nourishment — it is memory, culture, and an act of love."

Founded in 2009 by Chef Élodie Marchais, La Maison began as a modest bistro in Lagos with an audacious dream: to bring the soul of French cuisine to West Africa. Over fifteen years, we have earned our reputation through unwavering commitment to quality — sourcing the freshest local produce, partnering with artisan farmers, and marrying classical technique with bold, contemporary flavour.

Every evening, our team of twelve chefs crafts a menu that celebrates the seasons. We believe that great dining is theatre — intimate, surprising, and deeply personal.
